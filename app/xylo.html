Xylophone

Zining Wang, Jiongming Shi, Qiang Yu, Tianqi Li, Jiaming Cao


Automatically playing Xylophone is the ealiest subproject of the Music Workshop project. It started at the summer of 2013, creating the first successful project of Music Worshop in the summer of 2014. the success of this system encouraged us to explore more instruments' automatically playing. The xylophone also has a broad music range as well as the harp. So nearly it can play any music by input a music MIDI file in the computer. And the music played by it can be shown below.
